Welcome to MagicBean

MagicBean was created with the next generation of retail finance in mind. Retail vendors can be introduced to cryptocurrency’s many use cases with someone who has their best interests. MagicBean is an ecommerce site, crypto vault, browser addon and rewards program that will grow into a Decentralized Autonomous Organization as vendors see the possibilities. It’s all about the magic of MagicBean.

Business Solution

From Day 1 MagicBean can help retailers with customer engagement as we build the rewards system and DeFi products. We will put our crypto into the hands of so many people and so fast it will make heads spin, thus increasing our vendors ability to connect with buying customers. From there it is about building loyalty, expanding our base and improving relationships with great customer service. MagicBean involves customers with crypto without the need for a wallet and creates innovative business solutions for our vendors.
